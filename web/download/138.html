About Game

Dive into the world of Don't Starve: Pocket Edition, a successful adaptation of the hit PC game, now in the palm of your hands with over 6 million players globally. This game plunges you into an unyielding wilderness survival adventure teeming with science and magic. Navigate through a strange unexplored territory as Wilson, a brave Gentleman Scientist, whisked away and trapped in this wild, eerie world, scrambling to find his way back home.

How to play

  • As a player, take on the role of Wilson, who must survive in an unfamiliar land by exploiting his surroundings and the various creatures inhabiting it. Your job is to ensure Wilson survives by managing his health, hunger, and sanity against the many challenges and risks thrown at him. Gather resources, craft items, and structures that suit your survival tactic. Endure by any means necessary to unravel the mysteries of this peculiar realm, while battling strange creatures, treacherous conditions, and unexpected surprises along the way. Your mission? Help Wilson escape and find his way back home.

Features

  • 1. Vast, Mysterious World: Jump into an unexplored, random world brimming with unknown creatures and dangers. Every game creates a unique experience.
  • 2. Character Selection: Play as Wilson, our intrepid Gentleman Scientist or choose among other available characters with different abilities and traits.
  • 3. Crafting & Building: Harness your creativity with the ability to gather resources and craft items and structures to adapt to your survival style.
  • 4. Science & Magic: Experiment with an intricate system of Science and Magic to augment your strategies, survival capabilities, and gameplay.
  • 5. Twists & Turns: Engage with unpredictable moments that will challenge your survival skills and adaptability.
