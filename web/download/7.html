About Game

Gear up for KartRider Rush+, a memorable kart racing sensation enjoyed by over 300M global players. It's back, bigger, and better. This thrilling game boasts more style, diversified game modes, and an adrenaline rush like no other. Whether racing solo or taking on your friends, this game is charged with unique gameplay modes from the beloved KartRider universe. Secure your favorite characters, upgrade karts, slalom across the ranks and etch your name in the annals of racing legends.

How to play

  • KartRider Rush+ is chock-full of diverse modes tailor-made for hardcore racers. Chase glory as a lone wolf, or team up with fellow racers for leaderboard domination. Try your hand at the Speed Race, frantically drift to the finish line on challenging circuits or prove your mettle at the arcade mode. Buzzing with modes like Item Race, Infini-Boost, or Lucci Runner, you can experience an extra layer of high-octane thrills. Embrace the Ranked Mode, scale through the racing tiers from Bronze to Living Legend. The never-seen-before Story Mode invites you to join the good fight with Dao and friends to foil Pirate Captain Lodumani's evil plans. Lastly, beat the clock in the Time Trial and cement your legacy as the fastest racer.

Features

  • KartRider Rush+ boasts unprecedented kart racing visuals. Style your Racer in the trendiest outfits and accessories and go BOLD with a diverse selection of stylish and iconic Karts. Adorn your ride with chic decals and pets.
  • Experience racing on another level with over 45+ adrenaline-fueled race tracks. Whether it's dodging traffic in London Nights or resisting the biting chill of Shark’s Tomb, there's a unique challenge for every player.
  • Join the Club to team up with players from around the globe, complete quests collectively, customize your Hom and unwind with fun mini-games.
  • Real-time competitive multiplayer matches let you exhibit sporting dominance and prove what true speed is all about.
  • Optimized mobile steering; time your Nitro boosts for perfect drifts and leave opponents in your dust.
