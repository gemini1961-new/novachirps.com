About Game

Step into the broadcast world with the humorous Talking Tom & Ben News app. Be ready for some rib-tickling fun as Talking Tom and Talking Ben turn into charismatic news anchors, delivering your words in their unique style. Set in a lively TV studio, this app expands your interactive experience with digital news anchors. Watch the duo bring your words to life on the screen as they take turns to mimic you. From amusing banters to laugh-out-loud mishaps, this app promises moments of comedy.

How to play

  • To dive into the humour-filled broadcast universe, just speak to Tom and Ben and see them take turns mimicking what you have just said. For added hilarity, swipe or poke the screen and watch as they tumble off their chairs! Besides having them mimic you, the fun doesn't stop there. The app gives you the power to co-create the news. Simply upload a video or picture of your choice, and see how Tom and Ben react. Let their banter unfold around your content. For a personal touch, you can also record your own news story and share it with friends by email directly from the app. A fun and engaging way to communicate, this innovative app adds a twist to the conventional conversation methods making for a delightful social interaction platform.

Features

  • Talk to Tom and Ben - Have fun observing the two take turns in mimicking whatever you say.
  • Interactive Comedy - Tom and Ben tease each other in an entertaining manner bringing in waves of laughter.
  • Co-create the news - Upload your videos or photographs and witness amusing reactions.
  • Custom News Videos -Record, create and share your very own news stories.
  • No-Ads Option - The app allows you to get rid of ads with an in-app purchase for uninterrupted fun.
