About Game

Dive into the fabulous world of fashion and fun with My Talking Angela 2. This virtual pet game by Outfit7, creators of hit games like My Talking Tom series, brings daily glamour and entertainment alive. Players assist the stylish cat Angela in her bustling city life. The game lets you bond with Angela, help her pick her outfits, engage in numerous activities, and enjoy jet-setting travels. My Talking Angela 2 is all about fashion, food, travel, and galore. This game ingeniously mixes interactive elements with a compelling narrative that is sure to keep you coming back for more.

How to play

  • Playing My Talking Angela 2 is simple and straightforward. You can start your fashionable adventure by giving Angela some awesome hair, makeup, and fashion upgrades. Dress her up to match the latest trends and create amazing looks. Various mini-games and puzzles are integrated into the game to test your skills and reflexes. Epic activities like dancing, baking, and martial arts help keep Angela busy. You will also make delicious food and snacks for Angela and choose some truly exciting travel options. You can collect stickers and unlock new activities and outfits. The app enables personalization of content to enhance replayability, keeping users engaged. The game also offers in-app purchases and subscriptions that automatically renew unless cancelled. There are alternative options to access all functionalities of the app without spending real money.

Features

  • 1. My Talking Angela 2 offers a variety of hair, makeup, and fashion choices allowing players to express their creativity and style.
  • 2. Multiple activities and mini-games like dancing, baking, martial arts for an enriched gameplay experience.
  • 3. A cooking gameplay mechanic where players can prepare delectable food and snacks for Angela.
  • 4. Travel features enable the player to explore different locations and atmospheres.
  • 5. The all-new sticker collection feature adds a unique collectible aspect to the game.
