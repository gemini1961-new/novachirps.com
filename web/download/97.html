About Game

Dive into the world of Doodle Jump - an incredibly entertaining, engaging and wildly addictive mobile game that has taken the gaming world by storm. Crowned as one of the hottest mobile games of all time, it's hailed 'the best mobile game ever created' by Touch Arcade, and 'a perfect micro-game, insanely addictive, and deliciously replayable.' by Macworld. The game invites players on an endless journey upwards on a sheet of graph paper, offering rounds full of fun, frolic, and exhilaration.

How to play

  • Playing Doodle Jump is as easy as pie! You play as a cute, doodled alien with one simple goal - to climb higher and higher. The gameplay is endless and your progress purely depends on your ability to jump from one platform to another. Tilt your device to the left or right to control your alien's trajectory and tap the screen to shoot down any obstacles or enemies. Along your journey, you will mine various power-ups like jetpacks and propellers to fuel your ascent. You'll also have to navigate through different obstacles including UFOs, black holes, and an assortment of monstrous monsters. While you're at it, don't forget to collect bonuses and rewards from completed missions. It's not just about survival but also about agile movements, strategic planning, and fast thinking.

Features

  • Boasting a plethora of fantastical worlds like Ninja, Space, Jungle, Soccer, Underwater, Snow, Halloween, Frozen Ice, Easter, and Pirates for you to leap around in.
  • Loaded with an array of awesome power-ups like JET PACKs, PROPELLER HATS, ROCKETS, trampolines and more.
  • Spiced up with trippy obstacles like UFOs, black holes and a variety of monstrous monsters.
  • Featuring a diverse range of platforms (Broken, moving, disappearing, shifting, EXPLODING…) to test your dexterity and agility.
  • Integrated with global leaderboards and fun achievements. Challenge your friends and beat their scores for ultimate bragging rights.
