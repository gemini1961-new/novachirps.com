About Game

Step into the world of business with Supermarket Mania® - Match 3! You don't merely play a game; you embark on a thrilling puzzle adventure. Here, you get to open your supermarket and expand your grocery chain. Accompany Nikki, the leading character, back to her hometown, Tinsel Town, where it's up to you to contribute to the city's development by playing through addictive match-3 levels to grow your supermarket business.

The premise of the game is straightforward, yet mastering it would test your tactical abilities. You might find the easiness of matching three or more identical treats deceptive when it comes to beating the several challenging levels packed in the game. So, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to strategize every move and aim to reach the targets to keep the business booming.

How to play

  • The gameplay of Supermarket Mania® - Match 3 is quite intuitive but skillfully complex. You start each level with a grid of treats. Your fundamental task is to switch and match three or more identical treats to clear them off the grid. Be warned though, as the number of moves is restricted, and several targets need to be reached to progress to the next level. So, you need to use your moves wisely. In addition, cumbersome obstacles make the game even more challenging. Gon't fret! Drive over them with a turbo cart to clear the way. Use and combine special boosters and power-ups to achieve your goal faster. But remember, the ultimate goal is to become a supermarket master, opening a record number of grocery stores across the town.

Features

  • Firstly, you can roll through hundreds of challenging levels on the city map, always stirring up the excitement.
  • Secondly, swap and match treats to win specially powered chips, adding up to your gaming thrill.
  • Thirdly, the game allows you to use powerful boosters to defeat obstacles at a much quicker pace.
  • Fourthly, there's an exciting reward system where you can collect medals by completing levels, opening new chapters and gaining significant rewards.
  • Lastly but importantly, the game promotes socialization. You can compete with your friends and follow each other’s progress, fueling a sense of competition and camaraderie.
