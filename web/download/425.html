About Game

Dive into the deceptively simple world of Baldi's Basics Classic, a game inspired by the eerie edutainment titles of the 1990s. This horror-themed adventure is bizarrely entertaining with a retro aesthetic that belies its cunning gameplay. Its meta narrative lacks any genuine educational substance but skillfully presents an intriguing, strategic experience that will test your problem-solving skills to the limit.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to collect seven notebooks from within a labyrinth-like school while trying to evade the relentless Baldi. This unpredictable AI adversary becomes faster and more formidable the more notebooks you manage to gather, making Baldi's Basics an increasingly frenetic and challenging quest of wits and strategy.

How to play

  • Playing Baldi’s Basics Classic involves a strategic blend of resource management, careful navigation, and timing. The primary objective is to gather seven notebooks scattered throughout the school layout. While this may seem simple initially, Baldi, your principal antagonist, becomes progressively quicker with every notebook you find, meaning a winning strategy is crucial.
  • There are two game modes - Story and Endless. Story mode requires you to collect 7 notebooks and escape the school, with Baldi increasing in speed each time you find one. The challenge is simple but highly demanding. In Endless mode, you aim to collect as many notebooks as possible before being caught by Baldi. Every correctly solved problem in a notebook slows Baldi down, making longevity the key to success in this mode.
  • The most effective approach involves exploiting Baldi's friends to your advantage, sensibly managing items discovered around the school, and familiarizing yourself with the layout of the school for optimal navigation.

Features

  • Baldi’s Basics Classic offers a universally appealing touch screen control scheme with optional controller support, giving you flexibility to play on a variety of devices.
  • The game showcases an original and engaging concept, taking players back to the 90s with its retro, nostalgic aesthetic.
  • Strategic gameplay keeps players engaged, balancing the collection of notebooks, evasion from Baldi, and clever use of items and allies.
  • Two distinct game modes (Story and Endless) provide varying challenges and high replayability.
  • The unpredictability of the AI antagonist Baldi amplifies suspense and challenge, ensuring a unique experience every time you play.
