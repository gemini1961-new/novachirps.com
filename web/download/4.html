About Game

Take your place in the world of soccer with EA SPORTS FC™ MOBILE 24. The future of soccer is here and it's mobile. Train and build the ultimate dream team of legendary soccer stars and put them to the test against the world's best in this realistic and immersive football game. Whether you choose to play as Real Madrid from LALIGA EA SPORTS or Manchester City from the Premier League, be prepared for some incredible goals and intense matches. Join us in shaping the next generation of mobile soccer gaming.

How to play

  • Jump into the action with dynamic PvP modes including Head-to-Head, VS Attack and Manager Mode. Start off by collecting Player Items of world-class talents like Vini Jr., Erling Haaland, and others to help your team reach its full potential. Once you've assembled your squad, get ready to compete against top players in the 23/24 season. Personalize your players' skills and attributes, then strategize and tackle the competition on the pitch. With the Elite Shooting System, your impact players can make their mark, while the Power Shot, Hard Tackle, and Knock On controls add new ways to score and defend. Experience the authentic UCL broadcasting package, stadium art, official UCL ball, and the iconic trophy ceremony in the UCL Tournament Mode. Or use the customization options in the Locker Room to give each player on your roster their unique look from kit to boots.

Features

  • True player personality reflects authentic characteristics, with tangible attribute impact and animation diversity.
  • Dynamic game speed offers a mobile-first and tactile gaming experience.
  • Elite shooting system allows impact players to make their mark.
  • Immerse yourself with new dynamic cameras and impactful replays.
  • Fully licensed players, teams, and leagues that you can unlock and play throughout the season.
  • Localized UCL commentary to bring the most immersive match atmosphere.
