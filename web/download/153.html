About Game

Z Escape - Zombie Machine Gun is a high-stakes real-time strategy game that guarantees thrilling gameplay for everyone. The game challenges you to rescue your team from the onslaught of the walking dead. You are provided with a Gatling machine gun attached to a helicopter and your mission is to paint the town dead. The challenge lies in strategizing and making quick decisions to block the path of the zombies and save your team. Be warned! The zombies are deceased, they run fast, and they're hungry. So, every shot counts in your quest to become the best helicopter sniper.

How to play

  • At the start of the game, you are onboard a helicopter, in control of a Gatling machine gun. Waves of zombies approach your team members, and you have to take them down to save your squad. Your aim should be excellent; a perfect headshot could be the difference between salvation and annihilation. You can explode protective barrels and destroy bridges to disrupt the paths of oncoming deads, forcing them to take longer routes. Time is of the essence, and every second gained could lead to another team member saved. With each new level, unlock new guns and characters to aid your game. Continually adapt your strategy in real time to choose the right target and ensure the safety of your team.

Features

  • Z Escape - Zombie Machine Gun is packed full of special features. The game boasts stunning 3D graphics that make for an immersive gaming experience. With the highest number of horde invaders demonstrated in a smartphone game, it presents a unique challenge to players. The gameplay is fun, addictive, and yet easy to understand, suitable for both kids and adults alike. It also offers a range of guns and characters for you to unlock, enhancing the depth of the game. Lastly, the game is 100% free to play, offering unfiltered fun at no cost.
