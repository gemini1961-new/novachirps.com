About Game

Harness your inner gymnast with Flip Master, the ultimate trampoline game on mobile! This dynamic and exhilarating experience offers a challenge like no other, defying the laws of physics as you learn and master a dizzying array of moves. From frontflips and backflips to gainers and layouts, every jump and bounce will bring you one step closer to becoming the Master of Trampoline. Whether you're leaping on a backyard trampoline, at the gym, or even in a vibrant circus, Flip Master provides an adrenaline-fuelled journey guaranteed to entertain.

How to play

  • Venture into a variety of cool locations and select your jumping grounds - a cozy backyard, a professional gym, a competitive trampoline platform, a crazy circus, or even a jam-packed trampoline park! Unlock dangerous and spectacular skills, with over 10 tricks up your sleeve ready to wow the audience. Power up your moves, with frenzied boosts that will assist you in your flipping mastery. Whether you opt for the strength-inducing medicine ball or the bouncy foam cube, you're set for an action-packed trampoline party. Don’t forget to record your best moves and hilarious falls to show off your trampoline dominance to friends worldwide!

Features

  • 1. Variety of Cool Locations: Whether it's a backyard, a gym, a competitive trampoline, a frenetic circus, or a trampoline park, choose your preferred jumping locale!
  • 2. Skills and Tricks to Unlock: Master over 10 heart-stopping tricks, all at the palm of your hand.
  • 3. Crazy Power-ups: Choose from a myriad of power-ups such as medicine ball, the Foam Cube, or the Coin Rain for an exciting gold rush!
  • 4. Amazing Characters: Choose, upgrade, and customize your distinctive characters, each boasting unique physics.
  • 5. Show off Your Best Moves: Record your impressive stunts and side-splitting falls, and share them with friends around the globe.
