About Game

Welcome to Pull the Pin, a game that brings together satisfying graphics as well as innovative levels. This sensational and thrilling game provides a challenging field where you can test yourself, your intellect, and even your friends! Put yourself in direct head-on with physics and mind-boggling levels. Who will be the ultimate victor at the end?

The main concept is simple and magnetic: all balls must find their destiny in the pipe. The question is, can you strategize to pull out the pins in the right order to achieve that objective? The gravity is your best ally pulling the balls towards the pipe, but these pins are the obstacles, and they won’t make your win easy at all! However, with the right strategy and a clear head, nothing is impossible!

How to play

  • The gameplay of Pull the Pin revolves around the basic principle of gravity. The balls naturally fall due to gravity towards the pipe and your main objective is to remove the pins in the way so that all the balls can go into the pipe. Yet, the presence of the pins makes it complicated. It sounds simple, but don’t drop your guard, because the challenge escalates with quick progression!
  • Add another layer of complexity: sometimes there are colorless balls! Why is this important? As they make their way to the pipe, these colorless balls have to touch a colored ball so that they too can own some color - now that's a fun twist! So be cautious and remember, the right withdrawl of the pins is the key. You must ensure that the colorless balls touch the colored ones before they slip into the pipe. It’s a game where precision is pivotal, so consider the law of gravity, sequence, and timing while pulling those pins.

Features

  • 1. Innovative Gameplay: Integrating physics dynamics, the game provides an interesting and fresh play-through.
  • 2. Mind-bending Levels: The game boasts of levels that are not only fun but also challenging, paving the way for hours of engaging gaming experience.
  • 3. Social Element: They say two minds are always better than one! Compete against your friends to claim bragging rights.
  • 4. Aesthetic Visuals: One cannot forget to mention the pleasing graphics. The artwork is simple, and yet it provides an appealing look to the game.
  • 5. Addictive Challenges: Between different colored balls and cunningly placed pins, each level packs an addictive challenge that will keep you coming back for more.
