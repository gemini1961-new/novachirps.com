About Game

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of CALL OF DUTY®: Mobile Season 11, a thrilling and engaging addition to the renowned FPS multiplayer game franchise. Back with a bang and unfolding like never before, this dynamic game offers action-packed seasons that keep players hooked and yearning for more. Boasting console-quality HD gaming on your phone, customizable controls, exciting 3D graphics, and sound, this iconic game franchise delivers a gripping narrative and fun on-the-go, all for free.

New seasonal content updated monthly ensures that the game never gets old. Each season unveils new chapters in the CALL OF DUTY® universe story and a plethora of unique unlockable content. Play the game your way by customizing your loadouts with a variety of iconic operators, weapons, and outfits. From competitive ranked modes to sharpening your aim in social play, there's something for everyone in CALL OF DUTY®: MOBILE.

How to play

  • The main objective in the game is to squad up and battle other players in thrilling multiplayer modes such as Team Deathmatch, Domination, and Kill-Confirmed against exciting backdrops of iconic maps like Shipment, Raid, and Standoff. You can play this riveting FPS anytime and anywhere. With fast-paced 5v5 team deathmatches and scary zombies action, the exhilarating game caters to different players' preferences. You can even engage in sniper vs sniper battles, putting your tactical skills to the test. Share a sense of community and camaraderie as you join a clan and participate in Clan Wars, earning unique rewards for your gaming prowess.
  • To make the game even more enjoyable and accessible, CALL OF DUTY®: MOBILE offers download options to reduce the app's size. This nifty feature ensures you can enjoy the full game without worrying about the storage space. Download size is reduced, and additional options are available to allow players to decide what to download, such as HD resources, maps, weapons, and operators.

Features

  • The game boasts console-quality HD gaming on your phone, making portable gaming an exhilarating experience.
  • CALL OF DUTY®: MOBILE includes a plethora of multiplayer modes and iconic maps, ensuring never-ending excitement and play.
  • The game incorporates customizable loadouts, allowing players to choose from iconic operators, weapons, outfits, and more, reflecting their unique gaming style.
  • The game offers competitive and social play, which includes joining a Clan and participating in Clan Wars, fostering a stronger sense of community among players.
  • Innovative download options allow players to experience the full game without storage space concerns. Players decide what elements to download, such as HD resources, maps, weapons, or operators.
