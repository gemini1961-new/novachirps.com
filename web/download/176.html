About Game

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of The White Door, the brainchild of the makers of the renowned Cube Escape & Rusty Lake series. With every turn this suspenseful point-and-click adventure takes, you'll find yourself drawn into a story that unfolds within the restrictive regime of a Mental Health facility. You accompany Robert Hill, a man battling amnesia, as you help him navigate through a series of events filled with unexpected encounters in a bid to rekindle his memories.

How to play

  • Your journey in The White Door begins as Robert Hill wakes up in Rusty Lake's Mental Health facility, disoriented and without any trace of his past. The game employs an innovative split-screen gameplay where you participate in his daily routines and experience the eerie atmosphere around him. At the core of the game, your objective is to help him unlock his mind's locks by exploring the dreams he has at night. The game is filled with riddles and brain teasers that you need to solve in order to access fragments of Robert's past. The memories are unraveled in a playful way, revealing layer after layer of who Robert was, and perhaps, who he might still be. Keep in mind that every decision you make can influence the overall narrative, hence proceed carefully.

Features

  • The White Door boasts 'Pick-up-and-play' feature, making it easy to start yet captivatingly hard to stop. It offers an interactive storyline, where you walk through a daily routine and collect memories in an amusing way, padded with riddles and brain teasers. Experience Robert Hill's life in Rusty Lake's Mental Health facility in a unique split-screen adventure. The atmosphere is uniquely gripping, with each day in the facility bringing its own suspense and a variety of unexpected, surreal events. The immersive and haunting soundtrack composed by Victor Butzelaar, elevates the gaming experience. Moreover, The White Door holds special achievements and hidden secrets waiting to be unfolded, adding an additional layer of intrigue.
