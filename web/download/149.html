About Game

Survivalcraft 2 is an immersive and compelling survival game where players are marooned on the shores of an infinite blocky world. The game offers an unparalleled sandbox experience with virtually limitless possibilities for exploration, crafting, and construction. Players must mine resources, craft tools and weapons, make traps, grow plants and build shelters to survive against the harshness of the game's world, while also hunting and foraging for food and resources. Whether you're dodging predators, blowing up rock formations, crafting custom furniture or simply taking in the vast blocky landscapes, Survivalcraft 2 is a thrilling blend of survival and creativity that promises endless hours of challenging and rewarding gameplay.

How to play

  • Playing Survivalcraft 2 is all about strategy and survival instincts. To start, players must scour their environment for resources – everything from wood and stone to food and water. With their hard-earned resources, players can then craft tools and weapons to help them navigate and survive in the world. From making weapons for hunting and self-defence to crafting gardening equipment for crop cultivation – the crafted tools help the players in a myriad of ways. Building shelters from collected materials can protect the players from predators and adverse weather conditions. The game also offers the freedom of creating intricate electric devices and personalised furniture. Multiplayer feature lets you collaborate with your friends to build great wonders or engage in exciting co-op survival ventures. Overall, Survivalcraft 2 is an exciting game of survival, exploration, crafting, and construction, where strategy, creativity, and quick thinking can mean the difference between life and death.

Features

  • 1. Extensive Crafting Options: From basic survival tools to complex electric devices, craft everything you need to survive and thrive.
  • 2. Immersive Exploration: Set out on an endless journey in an infinite, procedurally generated blocky world, each time discovering new environments and resources.
  • 3. Dynamic Survival Mechanics: Hunt real-world animals, grow plants, build shelters, and fend off predators.
  • 4. Co-op Multiplayer: Team up with up to three friends in split-screen mode for collaborative building, exploration, and survival.
  • 5. Customisation: Tailor clothes, build custom furniture, paint structures, and design complex moving machines.
