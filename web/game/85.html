About Game

Immerse yourself in the world of Earn to Die, a thrilling mobile game that pits you against a horde of zombies in a post-apocalyptic world. Find yourself stranded in a barren desert amidst bloodthirsty zombies with nothing but a dilapidated car and scant cash. Your mission? Drive through the undead to get out alive. With over 200 million plays online, this revamped mobile version brings the excitement, fear and adrenaline rush right to your fingertips. Buckle up for a ride of your life!

Combine action, driving and strategic upgrades to enhance your zombie-fighting experience. Earn cash to upgrade your car, unlock and customize new vehicles. Beef up your armor, mount deadly weapons, or install boosters - do whatever it takes to survive the apocalypse.

How to play

  • In Earn to Die, the primary aim is to drive through hordes of the undead and make it out alive. Start with a rusty old car, upgrade it by earning cash for each zombie you knock down during your getaway. Use these earnings to make your vehicle durable against the undead onslaught or to get new and better cars. But that's not all. Further customize your vehicle with exciting upgrades like adding a gun turret, spike frame, or even a booster to plow through the undead with ease.
  • Progress through the game by navigating across the desert in the Story Mode, aiming to reach the rescue helicopter on the other side. Time your runs for the Leaderboards in Championship Mode, or take a different vehicular approach to smashing pumpkins in the fun-filled Halloween mode. Regardless of your choice, be ready for an action-packed adventure with nerve-wracking thrill and excitement.

Features

  • Immersive Story Mode: Journey across the deserted landscape in a gripping adventure during the zombie apocalypse.
  • Wide Selection Of Vehicles: From race cars to trucks and even school buses, choose your ride.
  • Customizable Upgrades: An array of upgrades to customize your vehicles, transform them from run-down cars to zombie-killing machines.
  • Zombie Ragdoll Physics: Smash into zombies and send them flying with a realistic ragdoll physics engine.
  • Diverse Gaming Modes: Compete for the best time in Championship Mode, or enjoy the Halloween Mode after surviving the apocalypse.
