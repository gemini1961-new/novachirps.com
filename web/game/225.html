About Game

Welcome to the world of Dislyte! This game thrusts players into an engrossing comic book universe where urban myths come to life. Venture through a captivating series of comics within an infinitely possible universe. Here, you can build a squad of superhuman Espers, empowered by the gods of myth, who are on a mission to fight the creatures hell-bent on destruction. The unsuspecting land of Grandis lies in ruins due to the chaos brought by 'Miracles', floating structures that emit godly sonic waves. The question lies, will you succumb to the allure of ultimate power or rise as a champion for the people? The answer is at your fingertips.

How to play

  • So how do you play Dislyte? Embarking on this thrilling journey can be as easy as being engrossed in your favorite comic books. Your main task involves assembling a team of Espers, who boast a host of godly powers. Each Esper is a potential powerhouse, with special abilities and characteristics that can turn the tide of battles. While treading through the comic strip quests, you might come across hidden secrets, exotic treasures, and powerful enemies. Strategic planning and judicious use of your Esper's abilities could tip the scales in your favor during these intense battles. However, the real magic lies in unearthing the comic strip stories and immersing yourself in the beautiful narrative of urban mythology. Use all the resources at your disposal, keep your senses about you and fight for the fate of the world!

Features

  • One of the enchanting features of Dislyte is the presence of ‘Urban Myth Comics', a brand-new genre of comics that beautifully portrays captivating stories about the universe of urban myths. The game also prides itself on promoting diversity and inclusivity, with a myriad of diverse characters that players can meet and become during their immersive journey. You’ll encounter the likes of a biker vixen Odin, a sophisticated-butler Anubis, and irresistibly fluffy Sphinx Esper. Furthermore, the community aspect of Dislyte is a significant feature, where you can put forward your opinions and share in-game experiences with like-minded enthusiasts. Lastly but certainly not least, the mesmerizing art and audio-visual effects that contribute to the stellar gameplay are worth appreciating!
