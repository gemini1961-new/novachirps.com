About Game

Enter the ring with Punch Bob, a game that is centric around an unlikely hero. Despite his size and easy-to-run-out-of-breath nature, our protagonist Bob is more than meets the eye. Buried beneath his hefty exterior lies the heart of a true warrior. Punch Bob mixes wrestling games with brain teasers in a unique way, challenging players to obliterate enemies onscreen solely using Bob's colossal body. Don't be fooled by Bob's slow movement and seemingly less intimidating appearance, our hero doesn't need weapons. He poses a threat so powerful that a mere touch from his massive body induces a heart-stopping fear in his enemies.

How to play

  • Punch Bob blends brain-teaser elements with the power-based gameplay of wrestling games. Your task: obliterate all enemies on a map by slamming Bob’s hefty body into them. It requires logic, reasoning, and a good grip on physics. Use your brain to plan Bob's maneuvers. Be careful of traps that lurk around the corners. You have to figure out the best way to launch Bob, taking advantage of his significant weight to wreak havoc among the enemies. Once you toss him, they’ll know that the fight is over. With each level escalation, you’ll face more challenging enemies and more complex map layouts. Keep practicing and don't shy away from replaying a level to get better at it. Remember, the goal is to solve every tricky puzzle and clear the battlefield at the first attempt.

Features

  • Punch Bob offers funny physics gameplay that's both challenging and hilarious. It has hundreds of logic games that serve as challenging brain teasers. The game breaks away from the traditional fighting mechanics and brings originality with Bob’s weaponless yet destructive trait. Add flavor to Bob’s persona with cool wrestler outfits. Apart from standard levels, Punch Bob also features amazing bonus levels that escalate the fun and provide an exhilarating gaming experience. If you're up for testing your logic skills while enjoying a unique take on wrestling games, Punch Bob is the perfect choice for you!
