About Game

Enter the captivating world of Brain Test: Tricky Puzzles, a game that will undoubtedly take you on a brain-boosting roller-coaster ride! This addictive free puzzle game presents a series of unique and tricky brain teasers designed to bend your brain and challenge your mind. It defies common sense and takes you through an engaging journey that promises heaps of fun and a revitalizing brain-pushing experience that you'll thoroughly enjoy!

This gaming masterwork is not just an ordinary IQ game. It is an enthralling extravaganza that you can enjoy with your friends, turning dull moments into an exciting intellectual adventure. The game undeniably stands among the very best the world has to offer, and if you're craving for some brain gymnastics, just have a go at it. This game comes as a part of the excellent Brain Test Series of games with more brain-popping puzzles like Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories, Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests, and many more.

How to play

  • This game is all about finding exceptional solutions for extraordinary riddles. Each level presents a challenging riddle that requires thinking out of the box to crack. Puzzles range from easy and relaxed to devilishly hard! If you are ready to face the challenge, download the game, open up to the first puzzle and start solving! You may find the tasks simple initially, but don't let that deceive you. The game quickly ramps up, throwing at you a barrage of tricky brain teasers designed to push your mental boundaries. In moments of bewilderment, remember, what you see may not always be the answer. Thinking from different perspectives might help you outwit the game. Brain Test: Tricky Puzzles is full of surprises and unexpected quizzes, making it a must-try for every puzzle enthusiast!
  • You don't have to worry about internet connectivity while playing this game. It is an offline game, providing endless fun even when you're not connected to the internet. Just pop open the game when you're bored or tense and refresh your mind with an immersive puzzle-solving experience. The game gives you a fantastic opportunity to challenge your friends, sharpen your minds together, and enjoy endless fun. With Brain Test: Tricky Puzzles, every moment is an intellectual adventure!

Features

  • Mind-bending Brain Teasers: You'll encounter tricky brain teasers that will keep you surprised and engaged.
