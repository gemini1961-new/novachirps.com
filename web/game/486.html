About Game

Embark on a nostalgic trip with PAC-MAN, the legendary retro arcade game that has been captivating gamers for decades. Updated for modern devices, this epic game brings back the classic PAC-MAN Fever with all-new modes, exciting mazes, and power-packed power-ups. Enjoy the ultimate leisure as you chomp through GHOSTS and gobble PAC-DOTS. With a revamped look and intuitive gameplay, PAC-MAN promises an immersive arcade experience right at your fingertips.

How to play

  • Playing PAC-MAN is as fun as it has always been. Begin your adventure in the CLASSIC 8-BIT ARCADE mode where the objective remains traversing through each maze, avoiding ghosts and eating as many PAC-DOTS as possible. For those yearning for a plot, the STORY MODE offers magnificent narratives across hundreds of mazes. Unleash special power-ups and dodge phenomenal obstacles to make your gameplay super exciting. ADVENTURE MODE brings exclusive limited-time theme-based events where you can gather rare skins. Aim for the ultimate glory by dominating the leaderboards in the competitive TOURNAMENT MODE. Not to forget, the weekly maze challenges and three dynamic difficulty modes keep the gameplay fresh and challenging.

Features

  • 1. Retro arcade action with the fun-filled CLASSIC 8-BIT ARCADE mode.
  • 2. Thrilling narratives through hundreds of original mazes in STORY MODE.
  • 3. Unique power-ups and exciting obstacles to enhance gameplay.
  • 4. Limited-time events and exclusive skins to collect in ADVENTURE MODE.
  • 5. Competitive leaderboards, weekly challenges and varying difficulty modes to test your skills in TOURNAMENT MODE.
  • 6. Personalize your game with unlockable customization skins for PAC-MAN, GHOSTS, and the Joystick.
