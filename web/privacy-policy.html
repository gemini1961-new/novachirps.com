We may use services such as Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. These tools collect non-identifying, aggregated data to improve content and usability.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personally identifiable information. Non-personal data may be shared with advertising or analytics partners for the purposes described in this policy.
