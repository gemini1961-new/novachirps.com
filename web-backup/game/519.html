About Game

Garten of Banban is a thrilling exploration game that delves into the mysteries and horrors of a seemingly ordinary kindergarten – Banban’s Kindergarten. As you navigate through its creepy corridors and eerie classrooms, you'll uncover the chilling truth that lies behind its innocent facades. This isn't just any kindergarten, as you'll see; the characters at the heart of this attraction are the Banban And Friends Gang, the quirky mascots that no kid can resist. Prepare to unravel the secrets and confront some of your deepest fears with Unternehmen.

How to play

  • Venturing into Banban’s Kindergarten, you start by exploring the forgotten corners of the kindergarten. Use your flying drone companion to navigate through the facility, while looking for clues and solving puzzles to progress in the game. You will encounter the Banban And Friends Gang, the establishment’s mascots and icons throughout your journey. They may not be as friendly as you think, though, so stay alert. A seemingly easy day at the kindergarten quickly turns into a nightmare, as you dig deeper into the mystery of the ghostly disappearance of students and teachers alike. As you progress, the disturbing truth slowly starts to reveal itself. You must stay alive and sane during this journey of horror and mystery! Moreover, always remember, you are far from being alone…

Features

  • Innovative Gameplay: Garten of Banban combines horror elements with intriguing exploration gameplay.
  • Unique Characters: Meet the infamous Banban And Friends Gang, the mascots that captivate every child’s heart.
  • Thrilling Mystery: Dive into the eerie and suspenseful story of Banban’s Kindergarten.
  • Fly with a Friend: Journey alongside your trusty drone companion. It will help in navigating the kindergarten and keep your loneliness at bay.
  • Gripping Storyline: Unravel the horrific truth behind the disappearance of children and teachers and dig deep to uncover the secrets of the kindergarten.
