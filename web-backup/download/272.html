About Game

From the creators of Candy Crush Saga, Pet Rescue Saga, and Bubble Witch 3 Saga comes a fabulous farming game - Farm Heroes Saga. This fun-filled, match-3 puzzle game takes you on an exciting adventure across the farm, with the cute Cropsies. Facing the notorious Rancid the Raccoon, your mission is to solve puzzles and save the farm. Match fruits and plants to level up, team up with the Farm Heroes, swap and collect crops, and prepare to defend your lands. Whether you're an ardent farmer or a puzzle enthusiast, Farm Heroes Saga offers a rich gaming experience to enthrall everyone.

How to play

  • Playing Farm Heroes Saga is a breeze, but equally challenging. Start by matching three or more Cropsies to progress through the game. Each level presents a unique match-3 puzzle, which gets challenging as you advance. You initially go from puzzle to puzzle, matching fruits and plants. You level up by saving the farm from Rancid the Raccoon and join forces with the Farm Heroes to swap, match, and collect the Cropsies. And if you're stuck, you can call your animal friends for help and use incredible boosters. There are also Hero Mode and Rancid Boss battles where you can earn Magic Beans or stop the fruit and plant thief. With Farm Club, you can recruit cute animals to get help in solving puzzles and, with your friends, you can have an enhanced farming experience. Also, don't forget to participate in unique Farm Teams events.

Features

  • Miles of Fun with Hundreds of Levels: Farm Heroes Saga comes with thousands of luscious levels and puzzles, keeping the game fresh and engaging.
  • Hero Mode: Chase your high score in Hero Mode. Match as many Cropsies before you run out of moves, gain Magic Beans to help you win the game.
  • Rancid Boss Battles: It's you vs Rancid the Raccoon. Solve challenging puzzles with matching skills to keep him at bay.
  • Farm Club: Not just matching, recruit cute animals to help you solve puzzles and blast powerful boosters to win.
  • Play with Friends: Team up with your buddies, chat and play with them to make your farming adventure more exciting.
