About Game

Immerse yourself in a world where the magic of Disney, Pixar, and STAR WARS™ comes to life in Disney Magic Kingdoms. This enchanting app lets you create, manage, and live out your own Disney Park, populated with over 300 beloved characters from 100 years of Disney’s colourful history. From The Little Mermaid and The Lion King to Toy Story and Frozen, this app brings together all your favourite stories into a single immersive platform, complete with exciting quests, iconic attractions, and bustling park activities.

How to play

  • To get started in Disney Magic Kingdoms, your first task is setting up a magical park, choosing from over 400+ exciting attractions based on popular sites from Disneyland and Disney World. The magic doesn't stop at park building, as this app also allows you to collect a wide range of characters and send them on quests, where they’ll fly with Peter Pan, swim with Ariel, or even navigate a galaxy far, far away. The app is regularly updated with live, limited-time events that bring new characters, attractions, and adventures, and you can also earn rewards through monthly and weekly special events. Lastly, you’ll need to protect your Kingdom from infamous Disney villains. Saving your Park from an evil curse and setting the Kingdom free provides a touch of thrilling action to this heartwarming app.

Features

  • Over 300 characters available to collect from across Disney’s history.
  • Over 400 real and unique Disney park attractions to choose from when building your dream park.
  • New content introduced on a regular basis offering live, limited-time events, with new characters, attractions, and adventures.
  • Action-packed battles against your favourite movie villains, adding an adrenaline rush to this enchanting app.
  • Accessibility to play offline: enjoy Disney Magic Kingdoms anywhere, anytime.
