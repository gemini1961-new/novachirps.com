About Game

Say hello to Words of Wonders! This isn't just another crossword game, it's a voyage of discovery, a test of your vocabulary, and a tour around the globe, all wrapped in one. As you grapple with the challenges of forming words from mere letters, you'll also uncover the world's hidden wonders - the famous seven, as well as some lesser-known gems. It's an exciting, educative exercise wrapped in fun-filled gameplay. Whether you're a word nerd aiming to expand your vocabulary, a travel enthusiast, or simply seeking an engaging and educational game, Words of Wonders has something for you!

How to play

  • The gameplay in Words of Wonders is as intriguing as its premise. You begin with a limited number of letters and the ultimate goal is to form as many words as possible from these initial clues. The words you form will eventually unveil the solution for a particular level, which are clues to various world wonders and fascinating cities. However, it's about more than just forming words. Sometimes you'll need to connect words to form the solution, sometimes you'll have to guess. You will need strategy - will you try and solve the puzzle immediately, or uncover one word at a time? Maybe you'll opt to find all words on a level to make it more challenging. Every puzzle you solve takes you on a novel journey, allowing you to explore different parts of the world, imbibe new words, and enjoy the thrill of successfully deciphering puzzles.

Features

  • The hallmark of Words of Wonders lies in its engaging Gameplay. Be it the strategic connections of words or discovering world wonders, the game invariably keeps you intellectually stimulated.
  • The game boasts of a Rich Database. Every level, every wonder is unique, ensuring that the game never gets monotonous.
  • One key feature is its Vocabulary Builder. The game encompasses an expansive range of words, constantly challenging and broadening your lexical knowledge.
  • With the Extra Words Discovery feature, you can take the gameplay up a notch. Find more words on a level and add an extra layer of challenge.
  • Then there is the Aesthetic Appeal. Simple yet beautiful game design ensures that the game is easy on the eyes while being progressively arduous in its gameplay.
