About Game

Discover the joy of colors with the Happy Color®: Coloring Book app. This digital art game blends coloring book puzzles and your favorite Marvel or Disney themes into one. Whether you’re seeking a therapeutic way to de-stress, looking to escape boredom, or need a creative break, Happy Color is your go-to app. With content catering to all ages and lifestyles, it serves as an everyday source of fun, relaxation, and artistic entertainment.

How to play

  • Playing Happy Color is easy and intuitive. Just choose a template that catches your eye and start a fulfilling paint by number game. Each image is divided into numbered areas. You simply need to select a color from your palette and fill in the corresponding numbered areas. With each filled area, see the image come alive. It is an interactive experience with a layering of fun and satisfaction. Collaborations with Marvel and Disney offer unique pictures to color, featuring your beloved superheroes and animated characters. You can also support a good cause while you paint as Happy Color has partnered with several charities worldwide. User experience is paramount - you can save and share your masterpieces or join in an engaging online community on Facebook and Instagram.

Features

  • 1. Easy to use: Just tap, color, and enjoy. It couldn’t be simpler!
  • 2. Exclusive content: With the partnerships with Marvel and Disney, get access to exclusive digital art that you won’t find anywhere else.
  • 3. Art for a good cause: Take pleasure in knowing that your coloring supports children’s charities and environmental protection organizations.
  • 4. Diversity in art: With art from all over the globe, you're immersed in a world of varied and inclusive images that are sure to keep you entertained.
  • 5. Therapeutic benefits: Feel the stress melt away as you color. Known to have a calming effect, Happy Color serves as an excellent tool for art therapy.
