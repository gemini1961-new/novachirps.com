About Game

The Battle Cats is not your average cat game. This oddly charming and weirdly enjoyable game takes you on a journey across time and space where you command an army of peculiar looking cats as they wage a full-scale war against a host of weird enemies. Not only do you get to develop your feline army without needing to register, but you can also test your strategic wits in the never-ending battle with ALL THE CATS!

How to play

  • Playing The Battle Cats is incredibly simple. The battle system allows you to command your cats with just a tap of your finger; select the cat you want to do battle for you and off they go! If your base is under threat from enemy invaders, fire the Cat Cannon to keep them at bay. Assemble the right Cat squad to overcome your adversaries and ultimately destroy their base! As you clear stages, you gain XP and items to help level up your cats. The levelling system is just as straightforward; reach level 10 and watch your cats evolve. Unlock their True Form and unravel what unique abilities they possess! The game offers hundreds of stages across three Story Mode adventures and Legend challenges, making it perfect for casual play.

Features

  • Super simple, yet addictive gameplay!
  • Easy controls make it fun for players of all ages.
  • Wide variety of cat characters to choose from.
  • Clear stages to gain XP and level up your cats.
  • Collect rare and exotic Cats to create the ultimate feline army.
  • Regular updates with new and improved features.
